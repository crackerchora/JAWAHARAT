Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum installment amount for the Golden Future Plan?

The minimum installment amount is Rs. 2,000 per month, payable in multiples of Rs. 1,000 (e.g., Rs. 2,000, Rs. 3,000, etc.).

How many installments do I need to pay?

The plan requires 11 monthly installments to complete the payment cycle and become eligible for redemption.

Can I pay more than one installment in a single month?

No, the plan only allows one installment payment per month. Multiple payments in a single month are not accepted.

What happens if I miss an installment payment?

If you miss an installment payment, the proportionate benefits offered under the plan will be reduced. It's important to make payments on time to receive full benefits.

Can I cancel the plan before completion?

Yes, you can pre-close or cancel the plan anytime. In such cases, you may purchase jewellery equal to the value of installments paid (without plan benefits) or request a refund of the total amount paid (without interest).

Are there any making charges for jewellery purchased under this plan?

No, all jewellery purchased under this plan is free from making charges/wastage charges. Only gold, stone charges, other material charges and applicable taxes will be charged.

What documents are required to enroll in the plan?

You need to provide a copy of photo ID proof (Aadhar, Passport, PAN Card, Driving License, Voter ID, etc.) and bank account details at the time of enrollment.

Can I nominate someone for my plan?

Yes, you can appoint a nominee at the time of enrollment by submitting the relevant documentation. In case of the account holder's death, the amount will be transferred to the nominee.
